Background

After the pandemic, more people are in need of a second job or part-time job to support themselves and their family. These people, who need part-time jobs or side hustles like college students or stay-at-home mothers need flexible schedules and easy jobs. On the other hand, other people, especially mothers, are getting busier and want to look for help with house cleaning, babysitting, and pet walking. In these situations, we can see an opportunity for service providers and service looking people.
